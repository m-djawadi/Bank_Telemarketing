The data relates to phone calls made as part of direct marketing campaigns by a Portuguese financial institution. 
Predicting whether a client will sign up for a term deposit is the classification objective.
Calls served as the foundation for the marketing campaigns.It was frequently necessary to make multiple contacts with the same client in order to determine whether the product (bank term deposit) would be subscribed ('yes') or not ('no').

Dataset from :
